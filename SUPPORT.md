# Support Guide

We're here to help you get the most out of QuickDraw!

## 💬 Community Support
For general questions, feature requests, and sharing your designs, please use our **GitHub Discussions**.

- [**General Q&A**](https://github.com/rajputdivyanshu81/QuickDraw/discussions/categories/q-a)
- [**Feature Requests**](https://github.com/rajputdivyanshu81/QuickDraw/discussions/categories/ideas)

## 🐛 Bug Reports
If you've found a technical issue or a bug, please open an **Issue** on GitHub. 

- [**Open a Bug Report**](https://github.com/rajputdivyanshu81/QuickDraw/issues/new?template=bug_report.md)

## 🛡️ Security
If you need to report a security vulnerability, please refer to our [**Security Policy**](SECURITY.md) and do not report it through public issues.
